How much does OpenAssistantGPT cost? OpenAssistantGPT offer many pricing plan but you can start using it for free.

Here's our four pricing plans:

Free Plan: Includes 1 chatbot, 1 crawler, 3 files, 500 messages, and support for $0 per month.

Hobby Plan: Includes 3 chatbot, 3 crawler, 9 files, unlimited messages, and support for $3 per month.

Basic Plan: Includes 9 chatbots , 9 crawlers, 27 files, unlimited messages, and premium support for $9 per month.

Pro Plan: Includes 27 chatbots, 27 crawlers, 81 files, unlimited messages, and premium support for $27 per month.

These plans are designed to cater to various user needs, from personal projects to professional use.
